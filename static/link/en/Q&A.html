WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED‘s background clearing partner which?

WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED’s quoting system is linked to 16 top investment banks around the world, including Deutsche Bank, Goldman Sachs and Citibank, which will make price bidding. What the customer sees on the platform is the best bid or selling price of the 16 quote makers.

What is WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED`s trading model?

WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED adopts the trader-free clearing mode. As a brokerage firm, we only add a spread to the offer as a fee. Each order of the customer on the platform will be automatically forwarded to the clearing partner. This ensures that there is no conflict of interest between us and our customers. We firmly believe that only no traders clearing model is the fairest model for customers.

What type of brokerage service does WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED offer?

WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED is an international forex brokerage firm. We bring the quotes from the foreign exchange market into the trading platform, and provide products in the form of foreign exchange margin to the investors for transaction. The customer may use their own trading strategies, analyze, judge and place orders on our MetaTrader 4 platform.

How to open WINCENT INTERNATIONAL INFORMATION COMPANY LIMITED real trading account?

It is simple and convenient to open a real account. Just click here, and fill in the form for opening account online according to the requirements. After submitting your application, you will automatically receive a confirmation email from our backstage. You need to provide any proof of your identity (such as a passport, or driver’s license) and proof of address (such as bank bills, water and electricity bills, or a letter from any government department).

How will i get into my trading account?

We accept the deposit by bank transfer and China UnionPay bank cards. Deposit by UnionPay card arrives instantly while remittance may taken 2-3 working days to arrive. Note that when you select bank transfer, please be sure to fill in your name and MT4 trading account in the column of transfer information. Use the UnionPay card for deposit, our background system will be in accordance with the market exchange rate of RMB automatically converted into US dollars, and then transfer into your trading account. Under international anti-money laundering regulations, we can not accept third-party deposits. The account name must be the same as the name of the transferee.

How will I withdraw the funds from the trading account?

You need to log into our customer backstage, and submit an application for withdrawal of fund to the backstage. Withdrawal into any UnionPay account may take one working day. Remittance payments will generally be transferred into your bank account within 3-5 working days. If you select to deposit by a UnionPay card, our system will, in accordance with the market exchange rate, change US dollars to RMB and transferred into your bank account.
