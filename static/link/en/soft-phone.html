Mobile software

   

     The Mt4 computer transaction customer terminal has integrated the functions of market charts, technical analysis and order placing. It is a software most widely used in foreign exchange transactions. At present, more than 70% of the brokerage firms and the banks from more than 30 countries around the world choose Mt4 as an online trading platform for automating the transaction process and eliminating the customers’ daily cumbersome custody of the market.

Features and functions

  • Advanced K line chart

  • Get all the traded varieties

  • Real-time tradable prices for all products

  • View and manage open position contracts, net values, profit and loss

  • Kinds/ Start/ Close orders, place an order and modify the order type
